Eva Amurri Engaged to Chef Ian Hock: Susan Sarandons Daughter Finds Love

July 2024 · 3 minute read

She has at last achieved her “Happily Eva After”!

Eva Amurri shared a romantic Instagram photo on Monday announcing her engagement to chef Ian Hock.

The 37-year-old daughter of director Franco Amurri and Oscar-winning actress Susan Sarandon flaunted her massive diamond sparkler as she shared a number of photos following the proposal in the romantic city of Paris.

She tweeted to her 211,000 followers, “Those who know us know so well what this time means to us.” “We are incredibly happy. I look forward to spending the rest of our days together.

Also, she included the words My Heart’s Been Borrowed And Yours Has Been Blue from Taylor Swift’s smash hit “Lover.” It all works out for the best when I get to be with you.


Eva hastily posted to her Instagram Stories to praise about Hock’s choice of ring, claiming that she is “totally dying” over her diamond ring.

Ian collaborated with @cms custom to create it, and OMG Christina, you outdid yourself! She wrote.

Eva stated that Hock proposed in the Rodin Museum’s garden, referring to it as her “most favourite institution in Paris.”

Eva expressed gratitude to her followers for their “wonderful sentiments of congrats” in a subsequent post on her Instagram Stories.

The author added, “One thing I’ve learned over the years is that a relationship’s success and health has much to do with your community raising you and your journey as a pair as it does with your bond as a couple.

We simply cannot wait to see what the future holds.

Eva posted several pictures of the couple cuddling up to one another before celebrating their engagement at La Fontaine de Mars on her Instagram carousel.

In the final image, Augusta Amurri, Eva’s half-sister, is shown pointing to the ring. Eva also provided a photo of herself with Augusta.


More than two years after she publicly acknowledged their relationship on her blog, Happily Eva After, the couple got engaged.

She wrote in January 2021, “I’m beyond glad to share with you that I have a lovely boyfriend in my life. He has a lot in common with me and is named Ian. He is very kind, creative, attractive, and humorous.

Eva acknowledged she hesitated to post a picture of Hock because he isn’t a well-known person, but she eventually couldn’t help showcasing the chef to her fans.

Eva divorced Kyle Martino, a former soccer player, in 2020 after a nine-year marriage.

Martino acknowledged he and Eva “sucked at being married” shortly after their divorce. Mateo, Major, and daughter Marlowe are the children of the couple.
