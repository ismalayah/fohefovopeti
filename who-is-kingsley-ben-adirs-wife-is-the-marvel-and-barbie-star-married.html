Who is Kingsley Ben-Adirs wife? Is the Marvel and Barbie star married?

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Kingsley Ben-Adir is having a great year.

Recommended Videos

The blossoming actor is already headlining Marvel’s latest Disney Plus show, and in a few short weeks, he’ll be hitting the big screen in one of the biggest releases of 2023. With so many exciting projects currently underway, and Bob Marley: One Love less than a year from release, people are increasingly interested in what the 37-year-old actor is up to.

Ben-Adir is breaking plenty of hearts during his time on Secret Invasion — more due to his character’s malicious aims than his eye-catching qualities — and some far more positive heartbreak is bound to follow with Barbie‘s release. He’s collecting fresh fans in droves, and the incoming masses have one vital question: Is the up-and-coming star married?

Is Kingsley Ben-Adir married?

Ben-Adir’s presence in two of 2023’s major releases is sure to help his career, leaving fans with hope that we’ll be seeing far more of him in the future. We already know he’s headed back to the big screen for next year’s Bob Marley biopic, and his stellar performance in Secret Invasion indicates he’s far from done in the spotlight.

Now questions are shifting away from Ben-Adir’s career and onto his love life. As the star collects more and more fans, they want to know if they’ve got a snowball’s chance in hell to fill the position, and — thus far, at least — things are looking good for hopeful fans.

Ben-Adir is among Hollywood’s more secretive stars, so little is known about his love life. Several romantic partners have been linked to him over the years, with rumors of a secret marriage even cropping up once or twice, but in reality, he appears to be single. Photos of Ben-Adir enjoying Wimbledon, and a few slices of pizza, with a lovely companion back in 2022 set off a firestorm, but nothing major appears to have come of the outing. If the star is in a relationship, he’s keeping it tightly under wraps, and he’s yet to tie the knot with his paramour.

As his career well and truly takes off in Hollywood, it will inarguably get more challenging for Ben-Adir to keep his love life on the down low. Fans are already clamoring for information on his romantic dalliances, and they’re not likely to lose interest anytime soon. For now, however, he’s able to keep his private life private, and that’s something we can’t help but support.
