Post Malones Set List for Summer 2023 Tour Revealed After First Shows | Music, Post Malone, Set L

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Post Malone is officially on tour again and we have the full set list from opening night!

The 28-year-old entertainer kicked off the ‘If Y’all Weren’t Here, I’d Be Crying’ tour on Saturday night (July 8) with a show at the Ruoff Music Center in Noblesville, IN.

The sold-out show was attended by over 24,000 fans and grossed over $2 million.

During the show, Post performed three new songs from his upcoming album, which is out on July 28. Every night of the tour leading up to the album, Post will premiere a new song.

The 26-date run will wrap up in mid-August.
